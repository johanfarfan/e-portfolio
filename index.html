About me


Greetings! My name is Johan Sebastian Farfan. I've created this space on the web to share my achievements and progress during my time studying at Salt Lake Community College.

I was born and raised in South America. I'm from the Northern region of Venezuela, I used to go to the beach to swim, share with my friends, relaxing at the sunsets, so I also consider myself Caribbean.

Thanks to my interest in learning about different cultures I consider myself an art appreciator, as I always look for beauty and artistic details wherever I go. A high sense of natural preservation and a respect for all kinds of lives defines me.

Sharing experiences is another of my passions, it helps me to educating myself while I'm able to teach any kind of knowledge I have collected.

I am currently studying Computer Sciences to specialize in Web Design and Networking. I think that technology is something that should be used to make our everyday life easier, helping us to organize our tasks and keep in track of every event being in control of everything around us.

Although I've already decided my path, I still feel I'm exploring activities, hobbies and places. It's incredible how much you can learn every day and there's always something new.
