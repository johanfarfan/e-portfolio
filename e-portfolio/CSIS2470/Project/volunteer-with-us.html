Donate Time

Each week people from our community volunteer for 122 hours in our food pantry and 177 hours in our Thrift Store. These volunteers are the reason we are able to serve so many people. We often receive compliments on the welcoming environment created by these amazing individuals who donate their time.



We rely on volunteers in all aspects of pantry food-handling, including transporting, accepting, sorting, storing, stocking, and assembling food orders. Volunteers also assist with intake work, meeting people and assessing their needs. Food Pantry volunteers require a commitment of at least 4 hours per week. At the Thrift Store, volunteers assist with our entire operation – sorting, transporting, stocking and maintenance.

We would love to talk to your club, church, class, or group about our amazing volunteer opportunities.

If you are interested in volunteering, please fill out the Volunteer Interest Form and we will respond promptly. If you prefer to phone, please call us at:

  • (801) 364-7765 to speak with Rachel Fischbein, Emergency Services Director for Food Pantry Requests or
  • (801) 359-8837 to speak with K.C. Owens, Thrift Store Manager for Thrift Store requests.
